Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 2 chopped bonless chicken breasts
  • 4 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp curry paste
  • 1/2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp minced ginger
  • 1/4 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped cauliflower
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil to 3500F.
  2. Add curry powder and curry paste and cook for 1 min.
  3. Add chopped vegetables, garlic and ginger to oil for 2 mins.
  4. Add chicken and fry for 5 mins.
  5. Add coconut milk and cook for 10 mins.
  6. Once chicken is cooked all the way through serve over rice and add parsley garnish if wanted.
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste.
